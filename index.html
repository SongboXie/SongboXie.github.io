I am a PhD Candidate in Physics at the University of Rochester. I am currently studying quantum entanglement under Prof. J. H. Eberly.

I received my B.S. degree in Physics in 2017 from the Zhiyuan College of Shanghai Jiao Tong University in China, supervised by Prof. Z. Lv.

My current researches cover the geometric quantification of entanglement in multi-qubit systems, bizarre behavior of entanglement in quantum dynamical systems, and the evaluation of entanglement with experimental measurements.
